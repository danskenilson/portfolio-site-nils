About

My Story

I discovered my passion for improving digital products on the job. I got thrown into cold water by being part of a group that had to build a news website for my company. Being a journalist, I was soon able to combine the editorial and product perspectives.

In my further education as a UX design, I discovered that my skills as a journalist, doing research and interviewing people, were quite useful. Both lead to a better understanding of how things work or shouldn’t work. To find out about that excites me and is my major motivation.
