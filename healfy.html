Healfy

What if you could find everything that matters to your wellbeing in one place? What would users need - and how would they like to manage it? To find out, I started the discovery of Healfy.

My process

Research

To discover what people really need I conducted interviews with some potential users

Ideation

My findings lead to user personas, user flows and feature requests - which built the ground for the sitemap

Wireframes

Now I was able to sketch out some ideas in low fidelity wireframes, created a mid fidelity prototype from them and challenged it in a first round of user testing to stay on the right path

Design

At this point I built the foundation of the user interface and set up the specifications in a design language system

Prototype

I created a design prototype to test with users if it works fine and how it might be improved

Conclusion

The concept of healfy only works when all three main parts of the product (workouts, medicine and self reflection) are connected. This is a big task, because every of the three product areas could be a product of its own and it therefore takes a lot of time to finish the scope of the MVP.
